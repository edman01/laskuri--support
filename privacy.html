Privacy Policy – Laskuri

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Data Controller

The data controller for the Laskuri app is the app developer.

Contact: edman.build@gmail.com

2. What Data the App Processes

Laskuri only processes data that the user enters in the app, such as:

  • Saved entry name
  • Calculated value
  • Date and time

3. Why Data Is Used

Data is used only for core app functionality:

  • Saving calculations
  • Displaying saved entries
  • Editing and deleting saved entries
  • Exporting and sharing data when requested by the user

4. Storage and Location of Data

Data is stored locally on the user’s device. Data is not sent to the developer’s servers by default.

5. Sharing with Third Parties

The app does not sell or disclose user data to third parties.

If the user chooses to export or share data, the data may be transferred to the app or service selected by the user. In those cases, the selected service’s own privacy policy also applies.

6. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Laskuri does not include in-app advertising and does not use its own behavioral tracking or profiling.

7. Data Retention

Data is stored on the device until the user deletes saved entries or uninstalls the app.

8. User Rights

Because data is stored on the user’s own device, users can view, edit, and delete their data at any time.

9. Children’s Privacy

The app is not specifically directed to children and does not independently collect personal data over a network.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be made available with the app.
