Tally Counter

Tally Counter, called Laskuri in Finnish, is a simple counting app for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch. It is designed for quick counting, saving results, and continuing counts across devices.

Features

  • Start a new count
  • Continue an active count
  • Name a count
  • Save counts for later
  • View, edit, and delete saved counts
  • Export saved counts as PDF or CSV
  • Share exported data
  • Use the app on iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch
  • Sync active count state, saved counts, and selected settings between iPhone and Apple Watch
  • Choose app language
  • Choose app appearance
  • Enable or disable sounds and haptics
